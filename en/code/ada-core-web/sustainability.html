Sustainability

What is sustainable clothing

Sustainable clothing refers to garments produced in ways that minimize negative environmental and social impacts throughout their lifecycle.

This includes the sourcing of eco-friendly materials, ethical labor practices, and reducing waste and pollution during production, distribution, and disposal.

How we do this?

All of our clothing uses eco-friendly material which is either organic, recycled or biodegrabale.

Our clothes are made to be durable and high quality making them last longer to reduce overconsumption and waste
