About Learn Pro

Since 2008, Learn Pro has been passionate about making tech education accessible, practical, and inspiring. Whether you’re just starting out or leveling up your skills, our expert-led courses in programming, cloud computing, AI, and cybersecurity are designed to help you thrive in today’s digital world.

Founded by Maya Thompson and Liam Reyes while working in the recruitment department for technology giant Formix, they noticed a gap in the technology training landscape as interviewees lacked the skills need to succeed in their tech business. So they decided to create their own technology training company to fill the gap. Welcome to Learn Pro!
